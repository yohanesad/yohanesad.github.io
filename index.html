Who I Am

Strategic HR Partner
Rooted in Real Operations

I am an HR Business Partner with over a decade of experience in the manufacturing sector, specializing in employee relations, performance management, and organizational development within the SCG (Siam Cement Group) ecosystem.

My career has been built on one principle: HR is only as effective as its alignment with the business. From designing onboarding programs as an HR Officer to now advising senior leadership as an Assistant Manager HRBP, I bring both operational depth and strategic perspective.

I understand labor law, I resolve complex disputes, I build policies that stick — and I do it all while keeping people at the center.
